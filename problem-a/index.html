Hobbes the Tiger

This is Hobbes the Tiger, one of the main characters from the comic strip Calvin and Hobbes. He is the best friend of Calvin, a six-year-old boy with a wild imagination. To everyone else, Hobbes is simply a stuffed animal, but to Calvin, Hobbes is a real tiger with thoughts, emotions, and an unsatiable desire for tuna.
